Social Bite

Social Bite are a national Social Enterprise in Scotland. Through a chain of cafes and restaurants we employ over 100 people many of whom have struggled with homelessness. We are the largest distributor of fresh free food to the homeless in the UK giving out over 100,000 items of food and hot drinks per year.
